Apple Pie

Description

Apple pie recipe for a sweet and tart pie. Granny Smith apples are highly recommended.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 7 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 4 tablespoons milk (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Mix sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon zest together.
  3. Line one crust in a 9-inch deep-dish pan. Layer 1/3 of the apples into pie crust. Sprinkle with sugar mixture and repeat until done. Sprinkle with lemon juice and dot with butter.
  4. Place second pie crust on top of the filling and flute the edges. Cut a few vents in the top crust and if you want a glazed appearance, brush with milk.
  5. Bake at 435 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes.
