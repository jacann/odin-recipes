Stone Soup

Description

I have spent way way way too long working on this stupid first project trying to figure out what recipe to make so we are just going with stone soup. Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 moderately sized rock (or a large rock, or a small rock, it does not matter at all)
  • Some water
  • Some carrots
  • Some onions
  • Some potatoes
  • Some barley

Steps

  1. Put a pot on a stove or campfire or something, I don't care just make it hot and it will work probably.
  2. Clean the stone and put it in the pot (you are gonna need a pot btw, hope it isn't too late).
  3. Cut up the potatoes and onions, then put em in the pot.
  4. Do the same thing, except (surprise) do it to the onions. Put it in the pot (with the barley while you're at it).
  5. Oh yeah put like some seasoning in, at very minimum salt and pepper but do more if you don't want to be sad.
  6. Don't forget to stir.
  7. Congrats. You have made the soup of your dreams. I am soooo happy for you.
